Polish Crepes (Nalesniki)

Polish crepes are very popular in Poland for their fruity lightness served with a side of whipped up cottage cheese. I know, it sounds weird. However; once you try these, you will realize how addicting they are.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk or non-dairy milk
  • 1/4 cup of lukewarm water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2T unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2tsp fine salt
  • 1T unsalted butter, for frying

Steps

  1. In a medium sized mixing bowl, combine flour, milk, water, eggs, butter and salt. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a crepe pan over medium heat and lightly coat with butter.
  3. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan. Immediately rotate the pan to swirl the batter around until it covers the whole pan. Flip when crepe is lightly brown on one side.
  4. Repeat with the remainder of batter. Serve with some jam and cottage cheese on the side!
